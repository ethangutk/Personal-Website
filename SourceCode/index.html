Born in the early 2000s, I grew up in Cleveland, Ohio. I had a traditional American upbringing until middle school, when my dad got a new job opportunity, and my family moved our stuff overseas to Manly, Australia. The small suburb outside of Sydney allowed me to expand my horizons and realize what life could offer. Over the three years, I was fortunate to visit over 20 countries over four continents. Eventually, in 2016, I moved back to my hometown, where I finished high school and enrolled in University where I currently reside.

During that time, especially the last eight years, computers and explicitly telling the computer what to do piqued my interest. So I started to learn low-level coding languages like Lua and run my scripts on Roblox’s game engine. Later on, as my skills increased, I made two very successful games, Outrageous Chase and Outrageous Blades, with 376,000 hits and 177,000 unique users, and got featured on the website's home page by the time I was 15 years old. Since then, I have been taking on many different personal and academic projects and developing my skills throughout the process.
